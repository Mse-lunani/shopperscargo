Welcome to Shoppers Cargo

Shoppers Cargo is an airfreight forwarder that offers a full range of air logistics solutions as well as streamlined visibility and monitoring via our groundbreaking information management system and forwarding specialists by offering a comprehensive range of international air cargo solutions and unbeatable levels of customer service.

Global Cargo Experts

Shoppers Cargo global airfreight teams are located worldwide and are always available to advise you. Their specialized, industry-focused approach will make certain your air cargo travels the safest, speediest and most cost-effective way possible.

Precision Freight Management

Our airfreight shipments are planned, controlled and measured according to Cargo procedures, so your shipments are monitored door-to-door within our network, giving you the information you need, when you need it.

Innovative Air solutions

Our suite of airfreight products has been designed to meet the challenges our customers face in today’s dynamic production and service environments. Talk to us today to make your air cargo movements more effective.
